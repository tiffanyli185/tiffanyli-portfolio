Overview

  • Designed low-noise potentiostat circuitry for a 3-electrode amperometric biosensor, applying the Randles–Sevcik relation to predict a 1.9 µA peak current response at physiological creatinine concentrations.
  • Integrated the potentiostat into a KiCad PCB alongside a screen-printed electrode interface for a portable creatinine biosensor, which placed 3rd in accuracy at SensUs 2025.
